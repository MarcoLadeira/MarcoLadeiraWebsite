Update

Portfolio redesign

I shipped a redesigned version of this site — cleaner, faster, and simpler than before.

March 30, 2026

I have just pushed a significant update to this portfolio. The changes are mostly about removal — stripping back visual noise that had accumulated over previous iterations and letting the content carry the weight instead.

On the visual side: grain texture overlays, magnetic hover effects, 3D tilt cards, and scroll progress dots are gone. Animations have been simplified from blur-heavy sequences to clean opacity and translate transitions. The result is a site that feels calmer and loads with less overhead.

The chatbot has also been substantially rebuilt. It was previously too focused on LinkedIn data and AI initiative mentions to the point of feeling like a press release. The new version has a smarter matching engine — keyword-weighted scoring with context awareness and follow-up handling — and responses that are shorter and more conversational. It actually answers questions clearly now rather than padding everything with statistics.

The site was already zero-dependency — no frameworks, no build tools, pure HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. This update keeps that intact while making the experience meaningfully cleaner.
