Selected for Fenergo's first AI initiative

I was selected to work on Fenergo's first AI initiative, bringing MCP tooling and LLM workflows into enterprise financial software.

January 12, 2026

After several months working on Fenergo's core platform, I was selected to join the company's first AI initiative. The focus is on bringing practical AI tooling into the enterprise workflow — not as a demo or proof of concept, but as something that works inside real production systems.

The work involves MCP-powered tooling, LLM integration for internal workflows, and the careful engineering required when AI sits close to compliance-heavy financial software. It is the kind of work where reliability, clear boundaries, and production judgment matter more than novelty.

This is a natural continuation of the direction I have been building toward — combining backend engineering experience with practical AI systems thinking. More to come as the initiative develops.
