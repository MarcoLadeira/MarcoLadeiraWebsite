AWS ML Specialty and where my AI focus is heading

I completed the AWS Certified Machine Learning - Specialty. The useful part was sharpening how I think about production AI systems, workflow design, and reliability.

March 14, 2026

3 min read

On this page

Back to updates

The certification gave more structure to ideas I was already working with: model integration, evaluation, data flow, and the engineering trade-offs that make machine learning useful in practice instead of just interesting in theory.

Why it mattered

It also reinforced the direction I want to keep pushing in my day-to-day work: practical AI systems, better workflow design, and tooling that can hold up in production.

Where the work is going

  • Practical model integration
  • Better workflow design
  • MCP-powered tools
  • Stronger boundaries and reliability
  • Production-minded experimentation

What I want to keep pushing

The direction I care about is not AI as spectacle. It is systems that are intelligent, but still grounded in clear engineering decisions and useful enough to hold up in real work.
